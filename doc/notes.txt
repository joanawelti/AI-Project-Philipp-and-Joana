## automatic learning #### scenario:one creature against a (modifiable) number of other creaturesaim: reach exit of dungeonwith use of potions## learning techniquereinforcement learning(maybe NN)## reinforcment learning# rewards (-10 bis 10)- reach exit +10- die -10- hit enemy +3- get hit by enemy -3- kill enemy +5# actions- attack- evade / evade to exit     - try to run to exit if possible     - if no safe available, just run away from enemy- get and use health potion(- get and use energy potion)# states- health points (low, medium, high)- energy points (low, medium, high)- distance to closest enemy (low, medium, high)(- distance to closest health potion- distance to closest energy potion)
